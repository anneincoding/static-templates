Big Six Shark Divers are a specialist shark diving safari and sardine run tour operator in South Africa and Mozambique. We offer the unique opportunity to scuba dive and/or freedive with a variety of the worlds largest sharks such as Great Whites, Makos, Blue Sharks, Seven Gill Cow Sharks, Tigers, Bulls, Duskys, Bronze Whalers, Oceanic Blacktips, Spinners, Whale Sharks and Mantas.
